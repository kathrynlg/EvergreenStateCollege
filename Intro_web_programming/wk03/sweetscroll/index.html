  • Large mixing bowl

  • Cooling rack

INGREDENTS fake, don't eat this

  • 65 grams of cocoa
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tbps butter
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/3 cup flour
  • 2 tbps sugar
  • topping of choice

PREPERATION

  1. Preheat oven to 350c
  2. grease pan / muffin tray
  3. sift flour
  4. beat eggs
  5. add all ingredients to mixing bowl
  6. mix on high for 4 minutes
  7. pour into cake pan / muffin tray

COOKING INSTRUCTIONS

  1. bake of 35-40m for cake
  2. bake of 12-15m for cupcakes
  3. use toothpick to test to ensure cake is completed
  4. place item onto cooling try when finished
